Property Research Listing Analysis Property Verification Comparative Review Data Organization
Project Objective

Research and organize suitable residential property listings according to defined buyer criteria and prepare the findings for efficient agent or client review.

Project Role

Property research support, listing-information review, criteria matching, data verification, comparison preparation, and administrative documentation.

Final Output

A structured property-research report containing listing details, property comparisons, source links, observations, and organized findings.

Research Workflow

Project Process

The workflow focused on matching listings to buyer requirements, reviewing publicly available property information, and organizing the results into an easy-to-review format.

Step 01

Review Search Criteria

Identified the target location, budget, property type, bedroom and bathroom requirements, amenities, and other buyer preferences.

Step 02

Research Available Listings

Reviewed publicly available real estate platforms to locate properties aligned with the established criteria.

Step 03

Verify Property Information

Reviewed pricing, address, property characteristics, amenities, listing status, and other available property details for consistency.

Step 04

Compare Suitable Options

Compared shortlisted properties based on price, features, location, size, availability, and alignment with the requested criteria.

Step 05

Organize Research Findings

Compiled the listing information, source links, property details, and observations into a structured comparison report.

Step 06

Prepare Administrative Summary

Prepared a concise summary so the agent or client could review suitable options without searching through multiple listing platforms.
